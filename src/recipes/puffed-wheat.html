Puffed Wheat Squares

Recipe From: Jenna Luchak

Serving Size: 20sqs

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 70 minutes

Step 1

In a large bowl pour puffed wheat and leave aside.

Step 2

In a medium sauce pan combine Brown sugar, corn syrup, butter, and cocoa.

Step 3

Cook over medium heat to melt ingredients together. Stir regularly.

Step 4

Once mixture reaches a rolling boil, boil the mixture for 1-2 minutes. Stir continuously.

Note: Boiling for closer to 1min will give you a gooey bar, 2 minutes wil be a more firm bar.

Step 5

Remove the pot from heat and pour over puffed wheat.

Step 6

Using a rubber spatula carefully stir until all puffed wheat is coated with chocolate.

Step 7

Transfer mixture into an ungreasy 9in x 9in pan. Firmly press the mixture down into the pan, until compacted and relatively flat.

Step 8

Firmly press the mixture down into the pan, until compacted and relatively flat.

Step 9

Let cool until room temperature (~1hr) before cutting into squares and serving. Enjoy!
